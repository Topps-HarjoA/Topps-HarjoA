Soldering and Electronics


In Unit 8 we learned about soldering and electronics by practicing soldering through two projects and learning the basics about electronics.

Blinker

The blinker was my first soldering project ever which turned out pretty good. Although I lost the video showing proving that it worked perfectly I guarantee that it does. The challenges that I faced while soldering was how to melt the solder with the soldering iron in the right place on the circuit board. In the end I found the experience to be really fun because it was something you can really zone out doing.
