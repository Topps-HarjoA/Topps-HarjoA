3-D Printing and Scanning


In this unit we learned how to 3-D scan people and 3-D printing using Fusion 360 and Meshmix for design and Makerbot to print.

Unit Reflection Questions

1. The 3D printing process that MakerBot Replicator uses is Fused Desposition Modeling (FDM) which is the additive manufacturing process in which plastic filament is heated and fed through an extruder to build your object layer by layer. The advantages of the FDM is that it is most widely used because it is perfect for consumers and great for making protypes. The FDM has the lowest resolution and accuracy when compared to SLA or SLS and is not the best option for printing complex designs or parts with intricate features. 2.
